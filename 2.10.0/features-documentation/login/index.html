Sign in on Sunbird is a seamless process. Any organization, recognized education or learning body or individual users can sign in on the portal. Successful sign in on the portal is notified to the user via an auto-generated email.

Step Screen
1. Click Login on the landing page of Sunbird Portal

Logging into Sunbird Portal

Step Screen
1. Enter your Username or Email ID or Phone number
2. Enter your Password
3. Click Login

Recovering Password

Step Screen
1. Click Forgot Password?

Recovering Password using Email ID

Step Screen
1. Enter your registered Email ID
2. Click Submit
1. You will receive an email to reset your password
2. Click the Link in the email to reset credentials
1. Enter the new password
2. Re-enter the same password to confirm
4. Click Submit. You are logged into the Sunbird portal

Recovering Password using Phone Number

Step Screen Screen
1. Enter your registered Phone Number
2. Click Submit
1. You will receive a One Time Password (OTP) on your registered phone number
1. Enter the OTP code sent to your mobile device
2. Click Login
